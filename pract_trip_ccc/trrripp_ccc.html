Our Rafting Trips

From gentle floats to roaring whitewater, there is a run here for every skill level.

Book a Trip

Scenic Float

Class I-II, half day, ages 6 and up

A relaxed paddle through calm water and easy ripples. This is a great choice for families and anyone who wants the scenery without the big waves. Bring a camera and watch for eagles along the canyon walls.

Canyon Adventure

Class III, full day, ages 12 and up

Our most popular trip. You will hit steady rapids with a few exciting drops, then break for lunch on a sandy beach before finishing the run. A good mix of action and rest for most groups.

Big Water Expedition

Class IV-V, two days, ages 16 and up

Built for experienced paddlers who want a real challenge. This two day trip covers the river's biggest rapids with a riverside camp overnight. Previous rafting experience is required.
